Contributing

Bug Reports

Open an issue on GitHub. Include your Blender version, addon version, and steps to reproduce. Attaching the .prim file (if shareable) is a big help.

Pull Requests

Fork the repo, make your changes in a branch, and open a PR against main. Keep changes focused — one fix or feature per PR.

Reverse Engineering

If you've figured out undocumented parts of the .prim or .borg format, open an issue or a PR with your findings.
